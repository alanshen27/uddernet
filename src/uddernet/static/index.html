About UdderNet

iGEM Le Rosey 2026 project for early mastitis detection in dairy herds.

MastitisMLP — neural network on 7 milk-sensor measurements.

ResNet-18 — classifies udder photos from the iGEN dataset.

Late fusion — blends both model outputs with image-led weighting (70/30 or 30/70).
